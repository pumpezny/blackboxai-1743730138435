Our Story

Our History

2010 - Founding

VOUS Church was founded in Miami with a small group of believers meeting in a living room, passionate about reaching the city with the Gospel.

2013 - First Location

We moved into our first dedicated space in downtown Miami, allowing us to host weekly services and community events.

2018 - Expansion

We launched our second campus in South Beach and began streaming services online, reaching people globally.

2023 - Present

Today, VOUS Church serves thousands weekly across multiple locations and continues to grow its impact in the community.
